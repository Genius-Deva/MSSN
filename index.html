About MSSN

The Muslim Students' Society of Nigeria (MSSN) OASOS is committed to nurturing students spiritually, academically, morally, and socially through Islamic values and community service.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To produce God-conscious Muslim students who excel academically, spiritually, morally, and socially while positively impacting society.

Our Mission

To promote Islamic values, academic excellence, unity, leadership, and community development through education, da'wah, and service.
